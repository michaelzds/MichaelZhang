The Art of Tennis

Tennis a personal hobby of mine. I also love to make tennis videos.

The beauty of tennis isn't quantifiable. It lies in the players, the games, and the times. While the number of ways players can perform a seemingly similar sequence of motions is enormous, is there a most optimized approach? The spectacular success of Roger Federer and his fluid strokes that mostly optimize the work of physical laws in the human body during the interaction between the racquet and the ball is a wonderful example.
