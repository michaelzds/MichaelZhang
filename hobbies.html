HOBBY

Tennis


I have been playing tennis since 4th grade. It is the sport that I value the most because it has taught me so many things through the years, and the most precious one is the sportsmanship of being gracious in victories and defeats. To me, tennis is more than winning those sets, or the game becomes a beat without melody. It is about playing the "right" way, valuing the game itself, and overcoming myself. In the long run, that approach has helped me to enjoy the games and to find myself in the big picture.
