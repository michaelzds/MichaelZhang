Journey

Summer, 2022

Los Angeles. City of stars.

Is it you moon,

Who longs to dance with me

In your world of quiet mystery.

----

Fall, 2021

Manhattan. Concrete jungle.

Henceforth, safe across the river,

I shall see forevermore

The spirit beyond waiting for me on the shore.

Hope and faith, born of the current

And the time gone before me

By their hands shall lead me home.

----

Florida, 2020.

Beyond this strange yet familiar place

Looms but the horror of the shade.

And yet the menace of virus

Finds and shall find the years unafraid.

----

Washington, D.C., 2019.

Stop leaving and you will arrive

Stop searching and you will see.

----

Orlando, Florida, United States. 2017.

This journey begins, however, in the heart and it now had been laid to my charge to keep my own heart free of holdback and restrain.

Where does it lead to?

I search for the answers which only the future would give me now.

----

Beijing, China. 2016.

I knew if I left, a part of me would stay. But I knew if I stayed, part of me would leave forever.
